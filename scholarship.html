MCC Scholarship

Financial support and mentorship for deserving Architecture students in Metro Manila.

Benefits

  • ₱10,000-15,000 per semester (grant)
  • 1:1 mentorship & chapter networking
  • E‑certificate & features on chapter channels

Eligibility

  • 3rd–4th year BS Architecture
  • Public universities within Metro Manila
  • Min. GWA: 2.5 (80%)
  • Dean/UAP adviser endorsement
  • Proof of financial need

Timeline

  • Call: November
  • Interviews: January
  • Awards: before Summer term

How to Apply

  1. Prepare: latest COR, grades summary, endorsement letter, financial‑need proof, short essay (≤300 words) on purpose & impact.
  2. Submit online via the chapter portal (Account ▶ Scholarship) or e‑mail all files as a single PDF.
  3. Attend a short panel interview (virtual/in‑person).
Apply Online Email Documents

Selection Process

  • Screening of eligibility & documents
  • Panel interview & rubric scoring
  • Committee recommendation & Board approval
